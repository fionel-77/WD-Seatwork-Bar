About Us

Prismatic Pearl started with three friends who one day decided that they would come together to create the special safe space they dreamed of when they were still fresh out of college. A place where you can be your most authentic self without having to worry about the judgement of others. Anyone can be the life of the party and we make sure no one will feel left out. Prismatic Pearl is here to offer comfortability, solidarity, exciting shows and of course, very fun drinks that's sure to knock your socks off. Join us for the fun!
